About Us

Flysk Tour is a leading recruitment and travel agency based in Ghana for the past sixteen years that specializes in passport, visa and recruitment for to destinations around the globe. We are a leader in organizing leisure and business travels for individuals, groups and business organizations. Our strength lies in the partnerships we have with many renowned Destination Management Companies (DMCs) around the world that enable us to provide the most affordable and genuine recruitment packages to our cherished clients. Our in-depth industry knowledge has attracted many corporate and even government institutions such as Ghana Armed Forces to patronize our services.
